Hannah's Political Navigation Game

Donald Trump vs. Hillary Clinton

Welcome to my game! This game tests your ability to navigate around while a presidential debate is going on. The scene is set at a debate between presidential candidates Donald Trump and Hillary Clinton.
Directions: when you first start the game, you begin in the center of the stage, also known as Location 0.
If you click the buttons "North", "South", "East", or "West", they will move you one space in either direction. You can also type in the command line the same commands as the buttons show. You may press the "Help" button at any time for assistance.
If you try to click the same direction button twice, then you will not be able to move any farther than you are.
A score will keep track of your progress. Press "Score Chart" at anytime to see what score you have.
If you click the "Inventory" button, you will be able to see all of the items you have gathered throughout the game.
Try to beat your high score next time and have fun!
